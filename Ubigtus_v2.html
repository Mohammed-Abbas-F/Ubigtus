FAQs

COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our office dismantling & IT scrap services.

What exactly does office dismantling involve? +
Office dismantling is the systematic breakdown and removal of all office components — modular workstations, cabin partitions, glass partitions, false ceilings, raised flooring, electrical fixtures, HVAC systems, plumbing, server racks and all IT infrastructure. Unlike simple furniture removal, dismantling requires skilled execution to protect the core building structure and ensure safe disconnection of integrated systems. We return the space to bare-shell or landlord-specified condition.
How long does a full office dismantling project take? +
Timeline depends on office size and complexity. As a general guide:

• 500–1,000 sq ft (small office): 1–2 days
• 2,000–3,000 sq ft (mid-size): 3–5 days
• 5,000–10,000 sq ft (large floor): 1–2 weeks
• Multi-floor / campus: custom timeline with phased execution

We can also deploy larger teams or work after-hours/weekends to meet urgent lease handover deadlines.
Do you offer a buyback on office assets? +
Yes — this is one of our key advantages. We assess and buy back modular workstations, chairs, conference tables, servers, laptops, desktops, UPS systems, batteries, ACs, and scrap metal from partitions and fixtures. The buyback value is offset against dismantling costs, often making the entire project net-zero or even putting money back in your pocket. Our valuations are transparent, market-linked and provided upfront in writing.
Is data security maintained during IT asset removal? +
Absolutely. All hard drives, SSDs, storage media and IT devices are handled under strict data destruction protocols. We provide asset handover documentation for every device collected. For businesses requiring certified data wiping or physical drive destruction, we can coordinate with certified data destruction partners and provide compliance certificates — critical for BFSI, healthcare and government clients.
What happens to dismantled materials and e-waste? +
We follow a zero-landfill approach. Furniture in good condition is resold or refurbished. Metal components (steel, copper, aluminium) go to authorised recyclers. All electronic waste is routed to responsible disposal channels. We provide GST invoices and material documentation for all projects. E-waste disposal certificates are subject to availability and applicable regulations.
Can you work after hours or on weekends? +
Yes. We understand that many corporates cannot afford disruption to active operations. We regularly schedule dismantling work during evenings, nights and weekends, fully coordinating with your facility management team and building access protocols. Just mention your preferred schedule during the site assessment and we'll plan accordingly.
Do you handle lease reinstatement requirements? +
Yes — lease reinstatement is one of our specialised services. We work to the exact specifications required by your landlord or property manager, whether that means bare shell condition, original state restoration, or partial reinstatement. We review your lease agreement's make-good clauses and ensure the handover passes landlord inspection. Bengaluru's major commercial property developers and park operators are familiar with our work quality.
